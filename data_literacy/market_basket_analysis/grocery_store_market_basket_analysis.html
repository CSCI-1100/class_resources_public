How Market Basket Analysis Works
1
Collect transaction data recording which items are purchased together in the same basket
2
Apply the Apriori algorithm to find frequent itemsets that appear in many transactions
3
Generate association rules showing which items predict the purchase of other items
4
Calculate support (frequency), confidence (reliability), and lift (strength) for each rule

Key Metrics in Market Basket Analysis
S
Support: The percentage of transactions containing both items A and B. Measures how frequently the itemset appears in the dataset.
C
Confidence: The likelihood that item B is purchased when item A is purchased. Calculated as Support(A,B)/Support(A).
L
Lift: How much more likely item B is purchased when A is purchased compared to when A is not purchased. Lift > 1 indicates a useful association.
