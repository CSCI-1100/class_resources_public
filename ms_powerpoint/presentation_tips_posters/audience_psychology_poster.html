Language Techniques That Work

🎯 Chunking Information

Signposting: "First... Second... Finally..."
Category Labels: Create mental filing systems
Transitions: "Now that we've covered X..."

✨ Highlighting Strategies

Verbal Emphasis: "This is crucial..."
Repetition: Key points 3 times, different ways
Questions: "What if I told you..."

🔗 Memory Anchors

Callbacks: Reference earlier points
Stories: Wrap concepts in narrative
Analogies: "Think of this like..."

💡 Emotional Engagement

Personal Stakes: "This affects you because..."
Surprise: Unexpected stats, counterintuitive facts
Social Proof: "85% of people..."

⚡ Attention Management

Peak-End Rule

People remember emotional peaks and how things end - make your climax and conclusion unforgettable

Visual Breaks

Change slide design, add images, or use videos every 7-10 minutes to reset attention

Interactive Elements

Use polls, questions, or physical movement to re-engage your audience actively

Progressive Disclosure

Reveal information sequentially to prevent cognitive overwhelm and maintain curiosity
