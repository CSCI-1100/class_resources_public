Module Overview

Understanding Intelligence in the Digital Age

Artificial Intelligence is transforming how we work, learn, and interact with technology. In this module, you'll explore the distinction between Analytic and Generative AI, understand the fundamentals of machine learning, and learn how to effectively communicate with AI systems.

From understanding neural networks through committee analogies to mastering the CLEAR framework for AI prompting, these resources will equip you with essential AI literacy skills for the modern world.

  

What You'll Learn

  • Analytic vs Generative AI applications
  • Three-step machine learning process
  • Neural networks and pattern recognition
  • CLEAR framework for effective AI prompting
  • Information literacy in the AI era
  • Critical evaluation of AI-generated content
  • AI-assisted development (see second activity)
